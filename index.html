Hong-Bin Chen

I am a postdoc at IHES mentored by Hugo Duminil-Copin.

In May 2022, I obtained my PhD in math from Courant Institute, NYU. I am fortunate to have been co-advised by Yuri Bakhtin and Jean-Christophe Mourrat.

Starting in September 2025, I will be a tenure-track assistant professor at NYU Shanghai, where I completed my undergraduate studies.

I am interested in probability theory and probabilistic models from statistical physics.

You can contact me via email at hbchen(at)ihes(dot)fr. My teaching experience, talks given, and other information can be found in my CV.
